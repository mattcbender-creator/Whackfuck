Front 9
Hole 1: Green is Hole
Once your ball lands and stays on the green, it counts as holed out.
Hole 2: Free Throw / Longest Drive
Your team gets one free throw on this hole: throw the ball from its current position (does not count as a stroke). Both partners throw and the team chooses the best lie.
This hole also features a longest drive contest (must land on the fairway).
Hole 3: Bowl Your Putt
Once your team reaches the green, leave your putters in the bag. You must bowl the ball into the hole.
Hole 4: Tee Flip
After both partners hit their shot, stand face-to-face and flip a tee. The player the tip points toward must play that shot. Repeat for every shot until the ball is on the green.
Hole 5: Closest to the Pin
The player whose ball ends up closest to the pin wins a prize.
Hole 6: Pick a Club
Each player chooses one club to use for all shots until reaching the green. Your partner may choose a different club.
Hole 7: Happy Gilmore / Throw Your Drive
Hit your drive Happy Gilmore-style (run-up swing). Use the provided hockey stick to putt. You may optionally throw your drive instead.
Hole 8: Draw a Driver / Longest Putt
Use the spinning wheel to determine the club you must tee off with. Once on the green, the longest putt wins a prize.
Hole 9: Twin Putter
Once on the green, you and your partner must use the provided putter at the same time.
Back 9
Hole 10: Green is Hole
Once your ball lands and stays on the green, it counts as holed out.
Hole 11: 4-Man Scramble
All four players play this hole together as a scramble. Tee off from your team's assigned tee blocks.
Hole 12: Longest Drive
Longest drive on the fairway wins a prize.
Hole 13: Make the Putt Twice
Both partners must make their putt from the same spot for the ball to count as holed.
Hole 14: No Irons / Wedges
You may only use driver, woods, hybrids, or putter on this hole.
Hole 15: Closest to the Pin
The player whose ball ends up closest to the pin wins a prize.
Hole 16: Dice
Roll the wooden die once per team and follow the instructions.
Hole 17: BunkerTube / Longest Drive
If your ball lands in the fairway bunkers on the right side of the fairway, transport it to the greenside bunker.
This hole also features a longest drive contest (must land on the fairway).
